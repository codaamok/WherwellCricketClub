Alton III v Wherwell (away, 06/07/2019)
Saturday’s game for KJM sponsored Wherwell looked a tough on at fourth placed Alton III’s played hosts. With vice captain and leading wicket taker Ollie Emslie away, skipper Robbie Skeates finally won a toss and elected to bat.
