Tichborne Park II v Wherwell (away, 19/05/2018)
The KJM Group Ltd sponsored Wherwell CC traveled to Tichborne Park looking to make it three wins out of the first three games. Wherwell v Ramsdell (home, 19/08/2017)
KJM sponsored Wherwell played at home against promotion rivals Ramsdell in a crucial match that would decide either teams’ chances of going up this season. S Taylor lost his 11th toss for the season and was put out to bat on a mildy damp wicket. Ramsdell had an impressive bowling attack with a double wicket maiden in the 6th over having Wherwell on the back foot. St Cross IV v Wherwell (away, 12/08/2017)
KJM sponsored Wherwell played at St Cross on a gloomy day and skipper S Taylor lost the toss again and was put out to bat on a mildly damp wicket.
